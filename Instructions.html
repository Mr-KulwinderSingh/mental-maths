Mental Maths!

Welcome to Mental Maths a game to play with the numbers, a general idea about the game is that it is based on the basic calculations and no hard maths formula is needed. Generally this game is played between two players to check their abilities how good they are in maths without using the calculator. But a solo player can also play. If you have ever watched UK's channel 4 show "Countdown" then you know what this game is. To get more information and rules please click on the given buttons, and enjoy the game.
